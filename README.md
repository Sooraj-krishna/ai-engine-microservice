### 1. **24/7 Automatic Website Monitoring**

**What It Does:**
Your website is checked continuously for problems—like having a guard watching your store 24/7.

**What It Catches:**

- Security vulnerabilities before hackers exploit them
- Broken links and error messages
- Slow loading pages that lose visitors
- Accessibility issues that exclude customers

**Your Benefit:** Issues are caught and fixed before they affect your business or customers.

---

### 2. **AI-Powered Automatic Fixes**

**What It Does:**
When issues are detected, advanced AI (Google Gemini) analyzes the problem and generates a fix—just like an experienced developer would.

**How It Ensures Safety:**

- Every fix is tested in an isolated environment first
- Multiple validation layers check for errors
- Changes are submitted for review (you can approve or reject)
- Automatic rollback if anything goes wrong

**Your Benefit:** Most issues are fixed within 24 hours, automatically, with no developer fees.

## How It Works

### Step 1: One-Time Integration

Your developer (or ours) integrates the AI Engine microservice into your website's backend. This takes 1-2 hours.

### Step 2: AI Learns Your Website

The system analyzes your website's structure, features, and technology stack.

### Step 3: Continuous Monitoring Begins

The AI starts monitoring 24/7 for issues, performance degradation, and improvement opportunities.

### Step 4: Automatic Maintenance

When issues are detected:

1. AI generates a fix
2. Fix is tested in a safe environment
3. You receive a notification (approve or auto-apply based on your settings)
4. Fix is deployed to your live site
5. AI monitors to ensure everything works

### Step 5: Monthly Reports

You receive easy-to-read monthly reports showing:

- Issues found and fixed
- Performance improvements
- Competitive analysis insights
- Recommendations for new features
